Self-Propelled Scissor Lift

Introducing SHA's state-of-the-art Self-Propelled Scissor Lift, model SHA-SL-30ES, designed to elevate your operations to new heights. With a remarkable capacity of 0-0.3 tons, this lift is specifically crafted for applications such as electrical and building roof maintenance. Let's delve into the details that make SHA-SL-30ES a standout choice.

This moving scissor lift operates seamlessly with a hydraulic lift drive powered by a reliable BATTERY source, ensuring efficient and flexible usage. The SHA-SL-30ES offers a substantial working height of 30 feet, supported by a robust platform area measuring 2.4 X 1.2 meters. With a Rated Loading Capacity of 300 Kg, this lift is designed to meet diverse lifting needs.

Features:

The construction of SHA-SL-30ES prioritizes durability and stability, with a Net Gross Weight of 2000, ensuring a reliable and secure operation. The elevating time of 60 seconds adds to the efficiency, making it a swift and practical choice for various applications.Our scissor lift comes equipped with handrails for enhanced safety during operations. The vibrant BLUE & WHITE color adds a touch of modernity to your workspace. The lift offers a Max. Lifting Height of 14 meters and a Min Lifting Height of 3 meters, providing versatility in applications.

  • 0-0.3 ton capacity.
  • Ideal for electrical and building roof maintenance.
  • Model: SHA-SL-30ES, a moving hydraulic lift.
  • 30 feet working height, 300 Kg loading capacity.
  • 2.4 X 1.2 MTR platform area, 2000 Net Gross Weight.
  • BATTERY-powered with 60-second elevating time.
  • Max. Lifting Height: 14 MTR, Min Lifting Height: 3MTR.
  • Suitable for lifting capacities of 250-1000 KGS, NEW condition, BLUE & WHITE color, and an approximate order value of 650,000.

Designed for lifting capacities ranging from 250 to 1000 KGS, this NEW scissor lift is tailored for the future. The approximate order value of 650,000 reflects the commitment to delivering quality and value to our customers.In terms of applications, the SHA-SL-30ES is a versatile solution for electrical and building roof maintenance, goods and passenger lifts, auto mobile showrooms, and loading and unloading from trucks. Its adaptability is further highlighted by features such as lifting height up to 40 feet, custom-built design, multi-utility functionality, and the option to choose between fixed and mobile types, including a platform with extension.
